About Me

Wife to Colin, Mum to Kate and Gemma, unemployed safety management and systems professional, certified cat lady, avid traveller, motorcyclist, obsessed MotoGP enthusiast and Marc Marquez stalker, wine connoisseur, amazing cook, cake decorator, artist, patchworker.


In 1999, Colin and I, along with Kate and Gemma set off around Australia for 6 months in an off-road camper trailer. In 2014, I spent 4 weeks on the back on my husbands motorcycle travelling from Maryborough, Queensland back to Perth. In 2015 we both spend 6 days riding Ducati motorcycles around Tuscany, Italy. In my travels around the globe with Colin we've visited Argentina, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Chile, Czech Republich, England, France, Germany, Indonesia, Italy, Liechtenstein, Malaysia, New Zealand, Peru, Spain, Switzerland, Thailand, The Netherlands and USA. My obsession with MotoGP has seen me travel to circuits in Phillip Island (2 times), Australia; Sepang (2 times), Malaysia; Jerez, Spain; Le Mans, France; Mugello, Italy; Catalunya, Spain; Assen, The Netherlands; Brno, Czech Republic and Spielberg, Austria. Next stop is Burriram, Thailand.


I have had a diverse work history. I've worked in the Government sector, Gold Mining, Engineering, Airconditioning, Wine, Day Spa, Rail and at an Airport. I have been a secretary, an accounts clerk, a cellar door salesperson, a beauty therapist, an auditor and a safety and systems professional. Web developer is next on the list!
