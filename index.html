About Me


Wife to Colin, Mum to Kate and Gemma and three furbabies, cats - Oliver and Hank and Charko - the dog, happy to be unemployed safety management and systems professional, crazy cat lady, avid traveller, motorcyclist, obsessed MotoGP enthusiast and Marc Marquez stalker, wine connoisseur, amazing cook, cake decorator, artist, patchworker.


In my travels around the globe with Colin we've visited Argentina, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Chile, Czech Republic, England, France, Germany, Indonesia, Italy, Liechtenstein, Malaysia, New Zealand, Peru, Spain, Switzerland, Thailand, The Netherlands and USA. My obsession with MotoGP has seen me travel to circuits in Phillip Island (2 times), Australia; Sepang (2 times), Malaysia; Jerez, Spain; Le Mans, France; Mugello, Italy; Catalunya, Spain; Assen, The Netherlands; Brno, Czech Republic and Spielberg, Austria. Next stop is Burriram, Thailand.


In 1999, Colin and I, along with Kate and Gemma set off around Australia for 6 months in an off-road camper trailer. In 2014, I spent 4 weeks on the back of Colin's motorcycle travelling from Maryborough, Queensland back to Perth. In 2015 we both spent 6 days riding Ducati motorcycles around Tuscany, Italy.


I have had a diverse work history. I've worked in the Government sector, Gold Mining, Engineering, Airconditioning, Wine, Day Spa, Rail and at an Airport.


I have been a secretary, an accounts clerk, a cellar door salesperson, a beauty therapist, an auditor and a safety and systems professional. Web developer is next on the list!
